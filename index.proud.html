7 Libria National Authem

Our Pride

National Anthem

All hail, Liberia, hail! (All hail!)
This glorious land of liberty
Shall long be ours.

Though new her name,
Green be her fame,[a]
And mighty be her powers

In joy and gladness
With our hearts united,
We'll shout the freedom
Of a race benighted,

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorious liberty,
By God's command!

All hail, Liberia, hail! (All hail!)
In union strong sucess is sure
We cannot fail!

With God above
Our rights to Prove
We will o'er prevail,
We will o'er prevail,

With heart and hand
Our country's cause defending
We'll meet the foe
With valor unpretending.

Long live Liberia, happy land!
A home of glorius liberty,
By God's command!
